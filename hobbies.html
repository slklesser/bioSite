One of Kristin's favorite hobbies is playing the electric piano. She loves the sounds that can be made just from one touch of a finger. Kristin also uses the piano during Music Therapy.

Kristin loves animals, especially her own dogs. Benny, the newest addition to our family, is about 2.5 years old now, but joined the family just before the pandemic started. Kristin has always loved to have dogs hanging out with her.

Since Kristin is cortically blind, she likes to listen to funny sounds. She has lots of TV shows and movies that she likes to watch that have music and sounds. She especially loves the voices of the minions from Despicable Me movies.

Kristin also loves to spend time with her sisters, of which she has two, Kyle and Kolby. Kristin loves to talk on the phone, especially with her sisters, which is funny, because generally, she only likes to talk on the phone to men in our family!!!
